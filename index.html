Team Purpose

Objectives of NGCN are as follows:

  • Understand local grand challenges
  • Create innovative contributions to and possible open-source technology-based solutions for problems related to the grand challenges
  • Understand and apply intercultural communication skills while collaborating on diverse teams
  • Understand and apply best practices in computer programming and agile development
  • Improve technological literacy as well as programming self-efficacy and identity

Regarding the above objectives, we're creating this website to inform the public of the lasting impacts of conflict in the MENA region through interactive modules including quizzes, a map, and other educational tools

Our Idea

Welcome to our website! In this website, you will learn about the Yemen Crisis and Iraq War and have the opportunity to test your knowledge. We’ve also included an interactive map so you can get a better idea of what has happened/is still going on in specific areas of Yemen and Iraq.

Our team decided to develop this website to inform the public of two major events that have greatly impacted the world, particularly the citizens of Yemen and Iraq. We chose these two events because we wanted to examine an event that is still ongoing (Yemen Crisis) and an event that has ended but still has lasting effects (Iraq War). We hope that by learning more about what’s going on in the world around us, our audience can gain more empathy and become better global citizens. We also encourage everyone to do what they can to support the citizens of Yemen and Iraq.
